Albert Pumarola

Computer Vision Researcher at Meta

I am a Research Scientist at Meta, working on efficient generative models and neural rendering. Prior to joining Meta, I received my PhD from the Barcelona Tech University in 2021. My thesis was on generative models and 3D reconstruction, advised by Francesc Moreno-Noguer and Alberto Sanfeliu.

My research interests lie in computer vision, with a focus on generative models and neural rendering for photorealistic virtual human avatars and 3D reconstruction.

Receiver of the Best Paper Award Honorable Mention ECCV'18.
